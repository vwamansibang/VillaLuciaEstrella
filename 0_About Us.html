Our Story

Founded on April 18, 2009, Villa Lucia Estrella Resort has been providing unforgettable vacation experiences for our guests. Nestled in the heart of a lush plantation in Occidental Mindoro, our resort offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Our mission is to create a tranquil and comfortable environment where guests can relax, rejuvenate, and create lasting memories.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities and services to cater to the diverse needs of our guests. From luxurious accommodations to top-notch amenities, we strive to offer the ultimate resort experience. Our dedication to providing exceptional hospitality has made us one of the most sought-after resorts in the region.

Villa Lucia Estrella Resort is surrounded by lush tropical gardens and offers a range of accommodations, including garden-view rooms, family suites, and cottages. Guests can enjoy our swimming pool, restaurant, and bar, We also organize special events and themed nights to ensure that our guests have a memorable and enjoyable stay.

The resort was initially established as a videoke bar by the founder, Norma Norella. Despite facing opposition from skeptics who believed it wouldn't be a profitable venture, the founder persisted and expanded the resort by adding a pool and charging an entrance fee for swimming. As the first resort of its kind in the Rizal District, Occidental Mindoro, Villa Lucia Estrella Resort quickly gained popularity among locals and tourists.

Over time, the founder were able to repay their initial loans, and they continued to reinvest in the resort, adding a kids' pool, Concordia cottage, mini-fishing pond, and guest rooms. Today, Villa Lucia Estrella Resort is a well-known and sustainable business in the southern part of Occidental Mindoro.

The name "Villa Lucia Estrella" is derived from the names of the founders' mothers, Lola Lucing and Lola Ester. The resort was named in their honor to ensure that future generations remember their ancestors. The names of the resort's cottages and guest rooms are also inspired by the family's history, representing the names of their grandparents and children.

With a rich history and a strong commitment to providing exceptional experiences, Villa Lucia Estrella Resort is the perfect destination for a memorable getaway.
